Toronto based band Strip Lacquer is like breathing through the suffocating grasp of a shiny leather glove.

Strip Lacquer is a Toronto based band Drawing from the likes of art rock experimentalism, industrial abrasiveness, and post-punk ethos – Strip Lacquer is an outfit that encompasses sharp emotional songwriting with nuanced abrasiveness/dissonance/ and a diverse sonic palette.

Their lyrics are often soused in confounding subject matters to the likes of desire, suppression, (?) and absurdity. When combined with a lab-scientist(?)/curious approach to sound experimentation, the outfit wraps their focused songwriting in a malicious yet vulnerable array of expression(?).

All of which contribute to a delicate and murky disposition that addresses an amoral existence–sometimes passionate, sometimes hedonistic, sometimes arduous/distressful–and our relationships with each other.
